Meet, Eat & Enjoy The True Taste

Taste in food refers to the sensory perception of flavor—sweet, sour, salty, bitter, or umami—detected by taste buds on the tongue when food is eaten. It is both the action of sampling food to check its flavor (verb) and the specific, distinguishing, or enjoyable quality (flavor) of that food
